DM Procedures (from Reddit.com)

Dungeoneering

1: Tick turn counter.
2: Mark light source usage if 6th turn.
3: Roll for encounters if 3rd turn.
4: Ask players what they want to do. Describe room/encounter/consequences of action.
5: Repeat.

Wilderness Exploration

1: Tick turn counter. (1 watch for normal terrain, 2 watches for difficult. 1 watch for all other activities.)
2: Roll for encounters if morning/evening.
3: Describe hex/encounter.
4: Ask if they want to forage, search, camp, or move on.
5: Repeat.

In Town

1: (On Arrival) Describe town as seen from afar. Provide Location List.
2: Ask what they want to do.
3:Tick turn counter
4:Roll encounter if 3rd turn. On a 2, roll a rumor. Describe.
5: Describe new location or outcome of action.
6: Repeat steps 2-5.
7: Remind players to sell treasure, deposit pips, and tot up XP before leaving town. Remember to apply 1% bank fee.

Combat

1:Roll initiative, unless surprise attack.
2: If enemies are outmatched, roll morale
3:Go through initiative; 1 move + 1 attack/gambit per character. Repeat until all enemies fled, dead, surrendered.
4:On death or flee, roll morale for all enemies. On first stat damage, roll morale for that enemy.
5: At end of fight, roll and mark usage on items.

Days's End

1: Give exhaustion if no watches were spent resting.
2: Calculate and charge hireling pay.
3: Roll weather.
4: Tick home upkeep tracker. If 30th day, charge 1% of home cost to player bank.
5: Tick warband tracker. If 7th day, charge 1000p to player bank.
6: Tick season tracker. If 3 months have passed, move to next season.
